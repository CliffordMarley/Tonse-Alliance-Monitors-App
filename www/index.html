Tonse Smart Monitor App is a mobile application that is built for the Tonse Alliance electoral monitors' community to allow them to capture station poll data and identify inconsistencies instantly. The data is transmitted to the Alliance's tally center where it can be scrutinised and validated.

The app is designed to be used only by a pre-selected, pre-authorised and pre-registered Tonse Alliance Monitors, hence does not include a self registration option.

Once your phone number has been registered by the Tonse Alliance Team, they will provide you with a login PIN. Install and open thhe application to begin setting up your account, then follow the below steps:


  1. Enter your registered phone number. ( Do not include 0 (Zero) )


Once setup, you will be redirected to a login page where you will have to enter your assigned password to login.

Once everything is setup, you can login to the application by entering the PIN which was given to you earlier on.

After poll station voting process has concluded, make sure you pay attention and take note of all final agreed upon figures since they are the ones you are going to fill into the application form fields.

Take great care yourself and make sure you input only correct figures into the application's digital tally sheet.
